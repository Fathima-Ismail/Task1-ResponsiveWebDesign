NEW DELHI

  • The History

    New Delhi has been the capital of various empires and played a significant role in shaping the culture of the region.

  • The Culture

    The culture of New Delhi is a vibrant blend of traditions, festivals, and art forms. From classical dance performances to modern art exhibitions, the city has a thriving cultural scene.

  • The Landmarks

    Some of the famous landmarks in New Delhi include the Red Fort, India Gate, Qutub Minar, and the Lotus Temple.

  • The Cuisine

    From street food like chaat and golgappa to exquisite Mughlai dishes, the city offers a culinary experience that caters to all tastes.

The City

New Delhi, the capital of India, is known for its rich history, diverse culture, and iconic landmarks. It serves as the seat of all three branches of the Government of India and is known for its bustling markets, historic sites, and vibrant street life.

Local Festivals

New Delhi hosts various festivals throughout the year, including Diwali, Holi, and Eid. These celebrations bring people together from different communities to share joy and cultural diversity.

Shopping
